What bStocks are (learned from Binance)

What they are
Tokenized US stocks — certificates, NOT direct share ownership. Issued by BTech Holdings (a Binance affiliate).
Backing
Each token 1:1 backed by a real share held with a regulated custodian. A Proof-of-Collateral page is published.
Chain
Standard BEP-20 tokens on BNB Chain — self-custody + DeFi compatible. Trade via the same /api/v3 spot endpoints as crypto.
Trading hours
24/7, weekends included — but the REAL US market is open only ~6.5h/day (09:30–16:00 New York). Off-hours prices go thin/gappy.
Fees
Zero conversion fee stock↔token; zero maker fee promo at launch; standard spot fees on secondary trades. Spreads still eat thin targets.
Minimum
Fractional from as little as $5 per trade.
Dividends / splits
Dividends auto-reinvest on the ex-date; splits & corporate actions processed automatically.
Launch set
CRCLB (Circle), NVDAB (NVIDIA), TSLAB (Tesla), MUB (Micron), SNDKB (SanDisk); SPCXB (SpaceX) pending its listing.
Eligibility
ADGM (Abu Dhabi) only. NOT offered to US persons.
Age / risk
Listed ~11 Jun 2026 — only ~3 weeks of history, one market regime. Any early result is directional, not proof.

Recommended next steps

Don't build the live rig yet. Step-1 is 🟡 marginal on ~3 weeks of one-regime data. That's a hypothesis, not an edge.
Let history accumulate. The product is only ~3 weeks old. Re-run this dashboard weekly; watch whether the best config holds up out-of-sample.
If it keeps holding → build the separate PAPER pipeline (data collector tagged in/out of market hours, paper engine, "beat SPY/QQQ" benchmark) and forward-test ~1 week before any talk of real money.
Before ever going live: add an earnings blackout (stocks gap ~10% on earnings) and confirm spreads on the exact names traded.
